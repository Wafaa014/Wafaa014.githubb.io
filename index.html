Hi. I'm Wafaa Mohammed.

I am a PhD student at the Language Technology Lab at the university of Amsterdam. I am proud to be supervised by Dr. Vlad Niculae. My Phd is part of the UTTER Project.

My main motivation for persuing research in language is that it is one of the most vital ways for humans to communicate. I am fascinated by the way we acquire language and how it is processed in our brains; especially for multilingual people. My research interests include language understanding, multilinguality, and multimodality.

In my free time, I like to read, travel, and experiene new things; I am always on for a new challenge :)
